Dermal filler is a substance that can be injected into skin to replace volume loss that occurs with aging or to enhance areas that may be lacking the ideal volume. Most fillers are made of hyaluronic acid. These are the safest and most natural looking and can always be dissolved if needed.

What dermal fillers can do?
-Plump thin lips
-Reshape the lips artistically
-Enhance shallow contours
-Soften facial creases and wrinkles (nasolabial folds, marionette lines, smoker lines)
-Improve the appearance of recessed scars
-Reconstruct contour (jawline, chin, cheeks) deformities in the face (nose )
-Decrease or remove the shadow of the lower lids (tear trough)

Anti Wrinkles Injection

The cosmetic form of botulinum toxin, sometimes referred to as "Botox" by patients, is a popular injectable that temporarily reduces or eliminates facial fine lines and wrinkles. The most commonly treated areas are frown lines, forehead creases, crow's feet near the eyes. Several other areas have been treated such as thick bands in the neck, thick jaw muscles, lip lines, gummy smiles and lifting the tip of the nose.

Botulinum toxin can be used to:
-Smooth crow's feet, forehead furrows, frown lines, lip lines and bunny lines
-Diminish neck bands
-Improve the appearance of skin dimpling of the chin
-Lift the corners of the mouth or nose tip
-Soften a square jawline
-Correct a gummy smile
-Lift the eyebrows (mini fox eyes )

Skin Rejuvenation

Skin rejuvenation includes various treatments that aim to restore your skin from any damage. Skin damage could be a result of sun exposure, an underlying health condition, or a normal sign of aging.

Certain treatment options are milder than an overall facelift and can also be used to treat specific areas. Some of the procedures of skin rejuvenation include:
- Chemical peel
- BioRePeel
- Microneedling
- Mesotherapy
